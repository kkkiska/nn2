About us
Nativelish is a great site for those who want to learn modern English in a simple, fun and free way. On this online resource, you can use interactive cards to learn new words and take exciting tests that are focused on the initial level of knowledge. Nativelish provides an opportunity to learn the features and differences of the language of English-speaking countries, as well as delve into their culture.
Cards
The most common words and phrases that English-speaking people use in everyday speech are presented on our website for learning. With our flashcards you will be able to learn new words in English quickly and efficiently.
Start test
The Nativelish resource provides tests in English for beginners. With these tasks, you will test how well you know the basic words and be able to learn new vocabulary.
